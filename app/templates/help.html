Main Tabs

  • Home: Live temperature and humidity, active alerts, quick actions, and recent events. Readings refresh automatically — the "Updated Xs ago" note shows how fresh they are.
  • Status: Hardware link health, sensor state, alarms, and output state (heater, fan, door, lock, candling light).
  • Hardware: Manual controls — lock, door, egg turner, camera, candling light, and the alarm test. Actions that affect the eggs ask for confirmation first.
  • Settings (profile menu, top right): targets, alert tolerances, device toggles, and Wi-Fi.

Status chips explained

  • Sensors: online/offline — whether the last DHT22 read succeeded. When offline, pages show the last known reading, clearly labelled.
  • Alarms: none / N active — alerts fire when a reading stays outside target ± tolerance, or the sensor stops responding.
  • Setup mode / Hotspot active — the device is broadcasting its own Wi-Fi ({{ ap_ssid_prefix }}-XXXX) for onboarding or recovery.
  • GPIO: mock — the app is running without real hardware (development mode); readings are simulated.

Troubleshooting: Wi-Fi

  • To change networks, open Settings → Network, scan, pick the new network, and enter its password.
  • If the device is unreachable, hold the setup button for {{ hold_seconds }} seconds — the buzzer chirps, and the device starts its own hotspot. Join it and open http://{{ ap_ip }}:8000.
  • After switching networks, your phone or laptop may still be on the old network — join the new one and reload the page.

Troubleshooting: sensor & alarm

  • Sensor offline: check the DHT22 wiring (data pin GPIO{{ dht_pin }}). The dashboard keeps showing the last good reading with its timestamp, never fake values.
  • Alarm sounding: the dashboard banner says why. Press Silence alarm to quiet the buzzer — the alert stays visible until the cause clears.
  • Too many alerts: widen the alert tolerances in Settings, or untick "Alarm Enabled" to disable the buzzer entirely (alerts still appear on screen).
  • Test it works: use Hardware → Test Alarm for a short chirp.

Candling eggs

  • Open Hardware and press Candle ON to light the candling LED, then Capture for a photo.
  • Automated analysis: POST /api/vision/candle runs the full pipeline (light on → capture → AI analysis → light off).
  • Remember to press Candle OFF when you finish manual inspection.

Forgot your password?

  • Hold the device's setup button for {{ hold_seconds }} seconds (the buzzer chirps when it registers).
  • Open Reset password and enter your username or email with a new password.
  • This works only while standing at the device — it is the proof that you own it.
